IT Operations Management

A bird’s eye view of your IT Operations is indispensable. With various components that make up this ecosystem, from the old legacy to the new cutting edge technologies, there are a lot of blind spot that could render your systems incapacitated. Using the proactive ServiceNow ITOM platform will mean you will be in control of the health, optimization and have a holistic view of your services. Binmile’s methodology can prevent outages by proactively identifying changes so that you are aware, if there are going to be any adverse effects on your infrastructure. It also aims to reduce event noise, offer insight into costs and keep your business policy compliant enterprise wide. A customized ITOM solution, can help you gain clarity into essential business services, infrastructure and also help improve the health of the services, and introduce automation that will make your systems further efficient.

Governance Risk and Compliance

Organizations are expanding, entering new geographies and even new sectors which they did not cater to earlier. Be it vendor risk management or compliance management. It provides you with real time dashboards for high risks areas, updates on non-compliance and audit finding. All this will enable you to make better and informed decisions resulting in performance enhancement of overall business. We at Binmile understand this crucial and important aspect of GRC. We help you in planning your GRC implementation by understanding and evaluating your business requirement and process.
